# Code of Conduct

This project follows a contributor-first standard of respectful, constructive collaboration.

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the Altertable community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Taking responsibility for mistakes and learning from them

Examples of unacceptable behavior include harassment, intimidation, discrimination, and sustained disruption of discussions.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact Altertable maintainers through the repository security or support channels listed in `SECURITY.md`.
